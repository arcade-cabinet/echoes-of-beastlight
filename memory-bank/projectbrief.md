# Echoes of Beastlight - Project Brief

## Overview

A mobile-first JRPG with procedural generation and monster taming mechanics. Every playthrough creates a unique world with unique creatures, capturing the magic of classic 16-bit era games (Secret of Mana, Chrono Trigger, Final Fantasy VI) while ensuring infinite replayability.

## Core Pillars

### 1. Procedural Nostalgia
- AI-driven world generation creates endless variety
- Consistent pixel art style (32x32 sprites, black outlines)
- Familiar gameplay patterns with infinite variation
- Shareable world seeds for community engagement

### 2. Monster Taming
- Discover, battle, and tame procedurally generated monsters
- Each world seed creates unique creatures with distinct abilities
- Evolution paths discovered through gameplay
- Build synergistic teams for turn-based combat

### 3. Classic JRPG Feel
- Turn-based combat with strategic depth
- Exploration with secrets and hidden areas
- Epic storyline that adapts to the unique world
- Deliberate pacing with accessible difficulty

## Target Platforms

- **Primary**: Android (mobile-first)
- **Secondary**: iOS, Web
- **Framework**: React Native + Expo + Babylon.js
